# Code of Conduct

WP HookLens follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, constructive, and patient.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming language.
- Respect different experience levels.
- Focus feedback on the work.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarity.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information
- Repeated disruption of project spaces

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
